v0.9.0 — security & UX refactor

• ModelRegistry now stored encrypted with AES-GCM (auto-migrates
  from previous plaintext storage on first launch).
• SHA-256 change confirmation: if you re-install a model under the
  same name with a different fingerprint, an explicit dialog warns
  you before replacing.
• Memory watchdog: warns before loading a model too heavy for your
  device (prevents OOM crashes on Gemma 4 E2B).
• Rename and swipe-to-delete your conversations.
• Detailed SECURITY.md: explicit posture on root detection,
  biometric trade-off, non-whitelisted models.
• Debug-only logger for prompts sent to the native runtime
  (kDebugMode).
